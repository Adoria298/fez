Fez is a LAN chat app, built with python3 and flask.

The name comes from a shortening of "flask messenger" to "fess", which sounds like "fez". Preferentially, it should be written as a proper noun, with a capital "F".

Development

Fez is developed on github.com, under the GNU GPL v3.0 licence. You can find more development information, including how to contribute your self, in the links below.
